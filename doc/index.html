Our Premium Rice Varieties

We offer a carefully curated selection of rice varieties to suit every need and preference

Basmati Rice

Long-grain aromatic rice known for its distinctive fragrance and delicate flavor. Perfect for biryani and pilaf.

Jasmine Rice

Premium aromatic rice with a subtle floral aroma. Soft, slightly sticky texture ideal for Asian cuisine.

Brown Rice

Whole grain rice with natural bran layer intact. Rich in nutrients, fiber, and essential minerals.

Steamed Rice

Parboiled rice with enhanced nutrition and extended shelf life. Non-sticky texture preferred for daily meals.

Premium White Rice

Polished long-grain rice with consistent quality. Versatile choice for everyday cooking and commercial use.

Super Kernel Rice

Extra-long grain rice with excellent cooking characteristics. Popular export variety with superior quality.
